How to play
  1. Choose a maze size and a number, then click New Maze.
  2. You start at the green entrance cell.
  3. Click an adjacent cell whose number is a multiple of the entrance number to move there.
  4. Keep navigating until you reach the red exit cell.
  5. Non-multiples are walls — you cannot step on them!
